Ewedu

Ingredients

  • Ewedu leaves
  • Kaun (potash)
  • crayfish powder
  • Iru (locust beans)
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Remove the leaves and set it aside
  2. Wash the leaves in clean water
  3. Put the washed leaves in a basket to drain excess water
  4. Cut the leaves into small pieces ( this makes it easy to mashes it with ewedu Brooms)
  5. Pour water in a pot
  6. Add Potash and allow it boil until the potash dissolves completely
  7. >Add locust beans into boiling water
  8. Put the sliced ewedu leaves
  9. Boil for 5-10 minutes (make sure you closely monitor it because it has a tendency to boil over and over flow)
  10. Stir Occasionally with a spoon to make sure it doesnâ€™t overflow
  11. After 10 minutes, put the pot of ewedu on a flat surface
  12. Mash with ewedu Brooms for about 5minutes
  13. Put ewedu soup back on the cooker
  14. Dissolve seasoning cube and salt in warm water and pour it into the ewedu soup
  15. Add crayfish powder
  16. Mix well and allow it cook for 2-3minutes
