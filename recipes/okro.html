Okro

Ingredients

  • 25 okra pieces 4 cups chopped
  • 1.5 cups shrimp
  • ▢1 smoked catfish
  • ½ cup palm oil
  • ½ onion
  • 2 tablespoon Iru (locust beans)
  • 2 mini bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon bouillon powder
  • teaspoon ground crayfish
  • teaspoon cayenne pepper or dry ground pepper
  • Meat stock can substitute with water

Steps

  1. Pour palm oil in a large pan on medium heat
  2. Add onions and stir fry for 2 minutes till it turns just a bit brown
  3. Add okra to palm oil and stir for about 5 minutes
  4. Pour in the meat stock from boiling the meat. You can add water if you don’t have any but might need to increase the spices added
  5. Add the meat, shrimp, bell pepper, smoked catfish and locust bean and bring to a boil. You can add or subtract anything thing on this list, just make it your own!
  6. Mix in the spices - black pepper, ground pepper, ground crayfish, bouillon powder, and salt
  7. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes if you’re ok with a coarser texture, or a few minutes longer if you want a finer texture
  8. Add more salt or spices if you desire a stronger flavor
