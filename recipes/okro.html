Ingredients

  • Red ball peppers (Tatashe)
  • Scotch bonnet chillies
  • Onions
  • Palm oil
  • locust beams (Iru woro)
  • Smoked Mackerel and Ponmo
  • Ground crayfish
  • Salt
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Get all your ingredients together
  2. Heat up the oil
  3. Fry the blended peppers
  4. Season and add the meat and spinach
